This is the only website you need to learn everything about coding

Why this website?

Because you're tired of switching between 10 tabs just to understand one topic. Road to Coder brings everything — tutorials, resources, cheat sheets, and project guides — into one simple, structured, and distraction-free place. Whether you're a beginner or brushing up your skills, this is your complete roadmap to becoming a confident coder

What will I learn?

From HTML to Advanced JavaScript, from backend development to deployment, and even Git, APIs, databases, and dev tools — you'll learn everything step-by-step with real resources taken from top YouTubers, blogs, books, and pro developers. No fluff, just what actually matters.

Is this free?

Yes — and always will be. This platform is built for learners, by a learner, to eliminate the confusion and chaos of finding trusted content. No logins, no hidden charges, just pure learning.

About Road to Coder

Created by a passionate self-taught developer, Road to Coder is a free learning hub that compiles the best of YouTube, blogs, and books — all in one place. It’s a journey and you’re invited to join it.
